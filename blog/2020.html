I graduated this year( All credits go to the pandemic. All the final semester exams were conducted online and managed to pass . It's important to figure out what you know and what don't know. Is that possible without honesty? I don't think so. Also, It's hard to offend an honest person(Give it a try).

Never spend this much time in my home after joining college. The one good thing about working from home is that you don't have to check bank balance frequently. But a big chunk of money in the bank doesn't give what you want in life. I don't have to pay any room rent only an emotional rent (It's huge).

Preparation phase of life is over. There are no more scheduled exams. I must be ready when I'm at the right time and right place. I don't want to make any boring mistakes again. The new year does not make anything better If actions are inconsistent. Tomorrow can give you a heads-up after that you have to burn some fuel.
